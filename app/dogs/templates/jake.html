A Bit More About Jake


Jake in a nutshell

Jake came to us in December of 2009 only he was Spunky back then. He was then and still tends to be the time-keeper. He is a great fan of the schedule and in the morning he will not let you for about it. His bread are the great herders of sheep and well... we are his sheep... (everyone needs a job). Jake is obedient.

Jake has graduated from his role of expert frisbee nabber. He does not chase frisbees or balls these days. He is mostly an indoor dog. But since Jen shaved his long coat, He will spend more time out of the house in the backyard. He doesn't walk on the leash much. But he does come with the family for our nightly walk around the neighborhood. Jake keeps things moving right along in the house. He knows his job and he does it. We think he's proud of the work he does and we are proud of him.
