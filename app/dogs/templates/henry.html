A Bit More About Henry


Henry in a nutshell

Henny, as we like to call him is a 60 pound Treeing Coonhound and THEY MUST be famous for there vocal abilities. When he wants something he will let you know it... until you do something about it. He is insistent and serious. He also loves to be scratched on the belly (sometimes) . He will let you know when the time is right. He came to us with Max on the 2-for deal. They told us they were best friends, but I am not sure about that. Henry came to us as a rescue from Kentucky. He is his own man. He likes to be alone for most of the day. When its time to eat, he will let you know. My advise is to get a pair of good ear plugs

Of late, henry is slowing down a bit. Sometimes he does not want to walk at night. And when Henry does not want to move, you will rupture something getting him to do otherwise. He is set in his ways and independent. He loves having his ears scratched. He moans and you feel so good knowing he is loving it so much. A word of caution. If you stop petting him before he wants you do... look out... because you will soon be getting a paw over your head, or in your eye, or someplace that could hurt. We love him to pieces. We love all of them.
