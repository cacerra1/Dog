A Bit More About Rosie- AKA Fefe


Rosie in a nutshell

Rosie came to us in October of 2007 at 7 months and only 1 and 3/4 pounds. So small she fit in her tiny food bowl at the time. She was all personality and the boss from the very start. It did not matter how big another dog was, she was putting him on notice, she was the queen. She loves to be with her mamas. She is good with being picked up. In fact she will scrape you something silly if you dont and she wants up... so watch your legs.

Rosie is a pushy lovable thing. She is filled with confidence. She goes to the bathroom anywhere she wants to. And that has nothing to do with being blind. She is the leader and she knows it. When she starts to bark, the big dogs follow right along. Her other nick name is "trouble in a 5-pound suit"... and believe me it fits. We love her... our fearless pack-leader. Proving to all shorties everything that size really doesn't matter.
